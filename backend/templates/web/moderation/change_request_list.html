ℹ️ About Change Requests

What are change requests?

Change requests allow users to propose modifications to song, album, or artist information. Moderators review and approve or reject these requests to maintain data quality.

Who can submit requests?

All authenticated users can submit change requests.

Who can review requests?

Only users with Moderator or Owner roles can review and approve change requests.
